Student Academic Support

This page is designed to assist students with NSFAS applications, bursaries, and university applications.

Early application is strongly recommended to avoid missing deadlines and improve chances of acceptance.

üéì NSFAS Applications

The National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS) provides funding for eligible South African students studying at public universities and TVET colleges.

  • Funding for tuition, accommodation, books, and meals
  • For students from low-income households
  • Online application process

Important:
NSFAS applications are currently closed. NSFAS will start communicating application results via the myNSFAS portal from 15 December 2025.

üîó Official NSFAS Website: https://www.nsfas.org.za

üíº Bursaries & Scholarships

Many organisations offer bursaries and scholarships for students based on academic performance, financial need, or field of study.

  • Government bursaries
  • Private company bursaries
  • University-specific funding
  • Field-specific bursaries (Science, Accounting, IT)

Important: Each bursary has different requirements and closing dates. Applying early increases your chances.

Applying to university early is critical, especially for competitive programmes such as Science, Accounting, and IT.

  • Prepare academic results early

  • Apply to multiple institutions if possible
